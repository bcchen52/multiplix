Frequently Asked Questions

What is QPM?

QPM stands for Questions Per Minute, similar to how WPM, Words Per Minute, measures performance used on typing tests.

QPM is used rather than a raw score to provide a uniform way to measure performance. There are also leaderboards for each specific time option.

How do I compete with others?

In order to have your scores displayed in your profile or ranked on the leaderboards, you must play with default settings. These settings are loaded in by default, and can be re-selected with the Reset to Default Settings. button.

What are the symbols in front of people's usernames?

Those symbols are the ranks, based on time on MultipliX.

Rank 1 - 10 minutes

Rank 2 - 1 hour

Rank 3 - 1 day
